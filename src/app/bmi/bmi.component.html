What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on a person's weight and height. It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters.

BMI Ranges

The following are the ranges for BMI:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: BMI of 30 or greater

It's important to note that these ranges are not definitive and other factors such as muscle mass and overall health should be considered when interpreting a person's BMI.
