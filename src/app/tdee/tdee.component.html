What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total amount of energy a person burns in a day. It takes into account your basal metabolic rate (BMR), which is the amount of energy your body burns at rest, as well as the energy burned through physical activity and digestion of food.

TDEE can be calculated using a variety of formulas and factors such as age, weight, height, and activity level. Knowing your TDEE can help you understand how many calories you need to consume in order to maintain, gain, or lose weight.
