Calories Burned by Physical Activity

Physical activity is important for maintaining a healthy weight and overall well-being. The number of calories burned during a specific activity can vary depending on factors such as weight, intensity, and duration. Here are approximate calorie burn rates for some common activities:

  • Walking (3.5 mph): 3.3 calories/minute
  • Running (5 mph): 9.8 calories/minute
  • Cycling (10-12 mph): 8.6 calories/minute
  • Sexual activity: 3-8 calories/minute

It's important to note that these are averages and actual calorie burn can vary greatly depending on the individual. Engaging in regular physical activity can help increase calorie burn and improve overall health and fitness.
